Please read given instructions carefully before starting the test.
In this test there are 30 questions and the duration of the test is 45 minutes .

  1. On commencing the online test, the timer shows the remaining duration of the test.

  2. Read questions carefully. For every question there are 4 options but only 1 will be correct.

  3. Color coded box on the left side of screen shows the status of the question.
    • Answered
    • Active
    • Mark for Review
    • Unanswered
  4. Click question number to view the respective question only.

  5. Check the appropriate answer and navigate to next quetion by clicking on next button.

  6. Once you have completed the online test you can proceed to click submit button to finish the test.
